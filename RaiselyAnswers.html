Tell us about a company who does customer support well, and why you think they're good.

There are actually a few companies that I believe have great customer service (from my interactions with them). Here are three of them in particular:

  • jetBlue Airlines (USA): When I was living in Chicago and traveling to Boston frequently (one of the jetBlue hubs), I never really had the chance to fly this airline until my fifth trip. I booked the ticket and realised that there was an error with my name on it. A quick tweet to their customer service page warrented a resolution, and I was happy enough to send them a tweet back asking if they gave away Pilots Wings on their flights (usually for kids but I was trying to disguise being for my cousin as I wanted them.) They sent me a friendly reply and made me feel welcome even though I was asking for random things. Their onboard service is also tailored to the customer- unlimited drinks, snacks and great In Flight Entertainment and WiFi, all served up with smiles. They are one of the only airlines I have flow that made me feel like a king rather than a statistic in their passengers. Taking this approach, especially in the United States, where aviation has become a race to the bottom, is the best way to keep a customer. While I do not have a chance to fly them anymore (currently), their treatment of me made me want to fly them even if they are more expensive than other carriers. This goes to show how their approach of the customer first, profit next helps to retain their customers.
  • Icelandair (Iceland): Icelandair is an airline based out of Iceland. While I have not had a chance to fly them, I had another Twitter interaction with them. I was seeing if they sold airline collectables (as I collect these and have it throughout my office - see this link for my collection!) They said they did not, but instead of giving me a textbook, dry reply to my query, they instead offered to send me a package for free from Iceland with some goodies in it. When the first package didn´t show up, they sent another one, again at no cost this approach to service, even though I wasn´t a paying customer, made me extremely happy and wanting to fly them at the first chance I got. That level of customer service, even though I wasn´t a paying customer, made me a convert and someone that would stand by their brand.
